How Access Calculation Works

Formula: daily_cost = monthly_rent ÷ 30  |  days_granted = amount_paid ÷ daily_cost
Example: Unit with KES 6,000/month rent → daily cost = KES 200. Tenant pays KES 1,000 → gets 5 days access.
Partial payments are always accepted and extend access from the current expiry date.
